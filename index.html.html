Google Forms — result collection
When a student submits their exam, results are silently sent to your Google Form and appear in a linked Google Sheet — visible to you in real time.
Follow the 4 steps below to connect your form.
1
Go to forms.google.com → create a new form → add these Short answer fields in this exact order:
Name  |  Roll Number  |  Exam Title  |  Total Score  |  Max Score  |  Percentage  |  Result  |  Correct  |  Wrong  |  Unattempted  |  Time Taken  |  Subject Scores  |  Tab Violations
2
Click the ⋮ menu → Get pre-filled link. Fill all fields with dummy text and click Get link. Copy that URL — it looks like:
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/XXXX.../viewform?usp=pp_url&entry.111=A&entry.222=B&...
3
Paste the pre-filled URL below. This site will extract your Form ID and all field entry IDs automatically.
4
Save & activate the exam. Results will flow to your Google Sheet the moment each student submits.
